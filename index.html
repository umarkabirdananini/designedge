About us

Design Edge Academy Training emerged as an extension of Design Edge Associates Ltd, a company renowned for its excellence in professional services within the construction and building industry. Recognising the growing need for enhanced professional capabilities in both private and public sectors, our organisation leverages its background in Computer-aided design software to empower individuals and agencies. Our primary focus lies in equipping professionals with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in their fields. Design Edge Academy is dedicated to delivering concise, intensive, and personalised training programs that introduce cutting-edge concepts and techniques through engaging and interactive methods. With a commitment to maintaining small, focused groups, we provide individualised attention and facilitate the practical application of theoretical knowledge. The Academy's core concentration is threefold: Professional Software Training, Computer Studies Training, and Skill Acquisition in Interior Design.
